A tactical roadmap to dominate the algorithm and double Coocaa's following.

1. The Algorithm Decoded (The Engine)

How do we get seen without spending millions on ads?

A. Recency & Relevance

The Concept: Instagram creates "waves" around trending audio. These waves crash after 48 hours.

The Strategy: We don't just post; we surf. We execute within the "Golden Window".

"We aren't just posting content; we are capitalizing on 'Velocity'. By hitting trending audio within the 48-hour Golden Window, we ride the free traffic wave."

B. The Retention Loop

The Concept: The algorithm's #1 metric is Time Spent. If they watch twice (loop), we go viral.

The Strategy: Create seamless visual loops where the end matches the beginning.

"We are designing for 'The Retention Loop'. By creating seamless visual loops, we trick the brain into watching twice. This signals 'high value' to the system."

C. Micro-Interactions (Saves > Likes)

The Concept: Likes are vanity. Saves are utility. A 'Save' tells the algorithm the content is valuable.

"We are shifting focus from Vanity Metrics to Utility Metrics. A 'Like' is a nod; a 'Save' is a commitment."

3. Incentivized Growth (The Candy)

A. Setup Wars (UGC)

We ask users to tag us in their "Worst TV Setup." Every person who posts is a free billboard.

"This is 'Weaponized UGC'. We generate thousands of organic impressions without spending a dollar on production."

"We won't release this code unless this post gets 5,000 shares." Turns individuals into a team.

"We are gamifying our growth. This creates an artificial viral spike that forces the algorithm to pay attention."
